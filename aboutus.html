ABOUT Imagine your own soul

WHO WE ARE

Our goal is to entice illustrators to come out and show their work, passion. Putting on canvas, digitally, your
thoughts, your feelings, serves to vent your artistic vein and talk to the world about what words can not be
transmitted.


“As an artist I think it is up to us to keep evolving and taking creative decisions,”
“Aiming to be innovative is something I feel I can dare to try every time I take a job, and that's amazing.”
“to be able to generate a communication method based on simplicity and synthesis, with few tools and colours.
I am interested in identifying people's behaviour, portraying activities, intimate moments, body gestures,
disfiguring objects and reducing the amount of detail in a composition.”
